# Working agreement

## Authority

- Record which actions agents may take autonomously and which require human judgment or approval.
- Treat [../state/vcs-policy.yaml](../state/vcs-policy.yaml) as the structured VCS authority.
- Preserve unknown or unrelated project changes until their ownership is resolved.

## Quality

- Define the proportionate checks required before a unit, milestone, or release is complete.
- Report verified evidence; do not claim completion from intent or partial output.

## Coordination

- Declare concurrent work in structured workstreams with paths, dependencies, and completion criteria.
- Escalate conflicts in shared policy, protocol, registry, or overlapping paths.
- Keep durable project truth here even when private memory or a conversation provides convenience.
